Data Support Technician II - Data Publishing

The mission of the Allen Institute is to unlock the complexities of bioscience and advance our knowledge to improve human health. Using an open science, multi-scale, team-oriented approach, the Allen Institute focuses on accelerating foundational research, developing standards and models, and cultivating new ideas to make a broad, transformational impact on science.

The mission of the Allen Institute for Brain Science is to accelerate the understanding of how the human brain works in health and disease. Using a big science approach, we generate useful public resources, drive technological and analytical advances, and discover fundamental brain properties through the integration of experiments, modeling, and theory.

We are seeking a detail-oriented Data Support Technician to join the Data & Technology team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing data transfer and digital publishing for our research projects and scientific products, ensuring that data is Findable, Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable. This position will interact regularly with scientists, IT staff, data engineers, and others to ensure that data is managed effectively and efficiently to support the institute’s research objectives.

The Allen Institute believes that team science significantly benefits from the participation of diverse voices, experiences, and backgrounds. High-quality science can only be produced when it includes different perspectives. We are committed to increasing diversity across every team and encourage people from all backgrounds to apply for this role.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

Work with research teams to ensure data is properly documented, organized, and prepared for transfer and publication
Package, transfer and publish data using appropriate protocols and standards
Audit data quality, integrity and availability and provide KPIs for data publishing
Researching and identify solutions to issues related to data formats, compression, and transfer
Manage data collections to ensure integrity and completeness, and ensure accurate tracking of data publishing, confirmation of availability, and validation of files for integrity
Collaborate with data archives, repositories, or other publishers to resolve issues and support process improvements
Collaborate with other software engineers to integrate data transfer processes and scripts with a services-based platform
Prioritize and manage open issues

Strong communication skills and customer focus
